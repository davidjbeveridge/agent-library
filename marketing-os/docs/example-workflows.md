# Example Workflows

## launch-saas

- Build context.
- Clarify audience and positioning.
- Produce launch SEO and page recommendations.
- Generate an experiment backlog.

## market-local-service

- Normalize the local business offer and buyer triggers.
- Improve trust signals and landing page clarity.
- Rank next actions with demand capture first.

## revive-stagnant-product

- Refresh context and bottlenecks.
- Summarize what is known versus assumed.
- Recommend the next highest-leverage experiments and setup gaps.
