# positioning-brief

## Purpose

Produce category framing, differentiation, proof points, and messaging strategy.

## Required Inputs

- business and offer context

## Optional Inputs

- competitor notes\n- customer voice\n- claims

## Outputs

- positioning statement\n- proof-led message map\n- risks

## Evidence Expectations

Use only the evidence present in the shared context and clearly mark assumptions where evidence is missing.

## Fallback Assumptions

When differentiation is weak, say so and recommend product or proof improvements before aggressive messaging.

## Validation Rules

- Do not fabricate metrics, competitor behavior, or customer sentiment.
- Downgrade confidence when evidence is weak.
- Produce implementation-ready notes when action is recommended.
